Welcome to PokéType! You can use this app to search for a Pokémon by name, id, or by using the dropdown Pokémon Explorer found below.
For a given request, the app will display the Pokémon's type(s), name, abilities, and column lists of its various type data.
A user may type in the Pokémon's name or id and selected the 'Go!' button to search.
If you are having trouble finding a Pokémon by name, try using their id or hyphenating their gender to their name (ex: "nidoran" will not be found, but "nidoran-m" or "nidonran-f" will be.

Still can't figure it out? Try the Pokémon explorer! You may select a Pokémon with your mouse or by typing in its name.
